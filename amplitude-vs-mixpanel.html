The case for Amplitude

These aren't positioning claims — they reflect deliberate platform decisions that matter at scale, especially for enterprises deploying AI products inside their business.

2026 — Industry First
01

You're building AI agents. Amplitude is the only platform that measures them as products.

Navi and Household Story are LLM-powered agents — non-deterministic software that generates free-form responses. Traditional analytics can tell you how many people opened Navi. It cannot tell you whether Navi actually answered correctly, why it failed, or whether a poor Navi session caused an agent to churn a policyholder 30 days later. That's the gap Amplitude Agent Analytics closes.

Agent Analytics decomposes every AI conversation into a structured hierarchy (Session → Turn → Span), applies configurable evaluators (task completion, hallucination rate, tool errors), and connects quality signals directly to downstream product metrics — retention, policy conversion, feature adoption. Amplitude found internally that users with high-quality agent sessions retained at 2.3× the rate of users who hit task failures. You need that signal for Navi.
